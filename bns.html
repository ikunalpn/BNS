Bhartiya Nyay Sanhita

The Bhartiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) is a proposed legal reform to modernize India's colonial-era criminal laws, replacing the Indian Penal Code (IPC). Its goal is to update outdated provisions, streamline legal processes, address emerging crimes like cybercrime, ensure a victim-centric approach, and enhance accountability in law enforcement. It aims to make the legal system more relevant, efficient, and responsive to modern-day needs, forming part of a broader effort to reform India's entire criminal justice system.
