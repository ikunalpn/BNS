Bhartiya Nyay Sahita (BNS)

Bhartiya Nyay Sahita (BNS) is a modern legal framework developed to replace the old colonial laws in India. It provides a comprehensive code for criminal justice and aims to simplify legal procedures while ensuring fairness and accessibility for all citizens.
